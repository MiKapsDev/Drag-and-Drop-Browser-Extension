Privacy Policy for Drag and Drop Emotes

Last Updated: May 18, 2025

The "Drag and Drop Emotes" Chrome extension does not collect, store, or share any personal user data. We only store user settings (e.g., whether certain emote types are enabled) and a local cache of emote texts (e.g., "Kappa") in your browser to improve performance. These data are stored locally using Chrome's storage API and are not transmitted to any third party.

We do not collect personally identifiable information, location data, browsing history, or any other sensitive data. The extension interacts with Twitch pages and emote APIs (7TV, BTTV, FFZ) solely to enable the drag-and-drop functionality.

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy, please contact me at mika.developing@gmail.com.
